Ongoing Personal Projects

Resume Optimizer

The Resume Optimizer helps users enhance their resumes by providing actionable feedback on formatting and content. It analyzes resume data to ensure alignment with industry standards and job requirements. This tool is ideal for job seekers aiming to increase their chances of landing interviews.

Options Screener

The Options Screener assists investors in filtering and identifying stock options based on customizable criteria. It leverages real-time data and algorithms to provide insights into market opportunities.

TV Show/Video Summarizer

The TV Show/Video Summarizer uses AI to generate concise summaries of television shows and videos, making it easier for users to catch up on content quickly. It processes audio and visual data to highlight key plot points and themes. This project is ideal for individuals who want to stay informed without spending extensive time watching entire episodes/videos.
